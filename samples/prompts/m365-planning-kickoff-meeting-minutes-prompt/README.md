# Planning or Initiative Kickoff Meeting Minutes



## Summary

This prompt generates structured minutes for a planning or initiative kickoff meeting, clearly defining the purpose, scope, and first steps of a new project or initiative.

## Prompt 💡

Generate the minutes of a planning or initiative kickoff meeting using the following structure:

- Initiative objective and brief context.
- Agreed scope (what is included and what is excluded).
- Key deliverables and success criteria.
- Roles and responsibilities of each participant.
- Key milestones and estimated dates.
- First actions to be taken after this meeting.

Use a professional and project-oriented tone.

## Description ℹ️

This prompt helps you capture structured minutes from a planning or initiative kickoff meeting in Microsoft Teams. It produces a clear summary covering the initiative objective, scope, deliverables, roles, milestones, and next steps using a professional and project-oriented tone.

## Instructions 📝

1. Ensure you have Copilot for Microsoft 365 enabled in your tenant.
2. Open the Microsoft Teams app.
3. Join or open the meeting where you need the minutes.
4. Access the Copilot app within Teams.
5. Paste the prompt in the Copilot app.
6. Review the generated minutes and share with participants.

### Improvise Usage 🚀

You can customize the structure to add additional sections like budget overview, risk considerations, or communication plan depending on your organization's needs.
